# Features Plan for Good Grounds Coffee Shop

Here are 15 detailed feature proposals to elevate the "Good Grounds" application to a premium, feature-rich platform.

## 1. "Coffee Matchmaker" Quiz

**Description:** An interactive, step-by-step quiz that asks users about their taste preferences (roast level, flavor notes, brewing method) and recommends the perfect coffee bean.
**Why:** Increases engagement and conversion for unsure customers.
**Tech:** React state, Framer Motion for transitions, specialized algorithm.

## 2. Interactive Brew Guides

**Description:** A dedicated educational section with step-by-step guides for popular brewing methods (V60, French Press, Aeropress, Espresso). Includes a **built-in timer/stopwatch** for each step.
**Why:** Positions the brand as an authority and helpful resource.
**UI:** Clean, large typography, timer overlay.

## 3. Loyalty & Rewards Dashboard

**Description:** A gamified user dashboard showing "Bean Points", membership tiers (Silver, Gold, Platinum), and redeemable rewards (free shipping, free mug).
**Why:** Encourages repeat purchases and brand loyalty.
**UI:** Progress bars, badge icons, unlock animations.

## 4. "The Daily Grind" Blog

**Description:** A content hub for articles about coffee origins, farmer stories, and tips.
**Why:** Improves SEO and brand storytelling.
**Tech:** Markdown rendering or simple JSON-based blog system initially.

## 5. Product Quick View

**Description:** A modal that pops up when hovering/clicking a product card, allowing users to see details and add to cart without leaving the current page.
**Why:** Streamlines the shopping experience.

## 6. Wishlist / Favorites

**Description:** Ability to "heart" items and view them in a dedicated "Saved for Later" page.
**Why:** Reduces cart abandonment and saves interest.

## 7. Store Locator (with Map)

**Description:** An interactive map showing physical cafe locations, popping up detailing hours and amenities (Wifi, Outdoor Seating).
**Why:** Connects digital to physical presence.

## 8. Dynamic Nutrition Calculator

**Description:** On product pages (especially drinks), show calculated caffeine, calories, and sugar based on selected size and add-ons (milk type, shots).
**Why:** Tranpsarency for health-conscious users.

## 9. Gift Card System

**Description:** A flow to purchase digital gift cards with custom designs and messages, sent via email.
**Why:** New revenue stream and user acquisition.

## 10. Coffee Bean Comparison Tool

**Description:** Select 2-3 beans to compare side-by-side (Roast, Acidity, Body, Price, Origin).
**Why:** Helps connoisseurs make informed choices.

## 11. User Account & Order History

**Description:** A comprehensive profile section to manage addresses, payment methods, and view past order status/receipts.
**Why:** Essential for a mature e-commerce platform.

## 12. Events & Workshops Calendar

**Description:** A schedule of upcoming coffee tastings, barista workshops, or live music at the cafe.
**Why:** Community building.

## 13. FAQ Help Center

**Description:** A searchable Accordion-style FAQ section for Shipment, Returns, and Coffee questions.
**Why:** Reduces support queries.

## 14. Referral Program ("Brew Cares")

**Description:** "Give a Cup, Get a Cup" referral system.
**Why:** Viral growth.

## 15. "Dark Roast" Mode (Dark Mode)

**Description:** A thematic toggle to switch the site to a sleek, dark aesthetic (using deep browns and blacks).
**Why:** Accessibility and aesthetic preference.
